Nike Store Return & Exchange Policy

1. Eligibility
- Items can be returned or exchanged within 14 days of delivery.
- Products must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging with tags attached.
- Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required.
- Final sale items (marked “Clearance” or “Promo Exclusion”) cannot be returned.

2. Refunds
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7 business days of approval.
- If the original payment method is unavailable, store credit will be provided.
- Shipping fees are non-refundable.

3. Exchanges
- Size and color exchanges are allowed within the 14-day return window.
- Exchanges depend on product availability.
- If the requested size or color is out of stock, you may choose a refund or store credit.

4. Defective or Damaged Items
- Defective or damaged products are eligible for free replacement or full refund.
- Customers must notify support within 7 days of receiving the defective item.

5. Cancellations
- Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of purchase if they have not been shipped.
- After shipment, the order must go through the return process.

6. Warranty Claims
- Footwear and apparel carry a 6-month manufacturing warranty.
- Warranty claims are reviewed case by case. Physical damage or wear & tear are not covered.

7. How to Start a Return
- Contact our support team via email (support@nikestore.com) or phone (+92-XXX-XXXXXXX).
- Provide your order number and reason for return.
- Pack the item securely with original packaging and drop it off at the nearest Nike store or request a courier pickup (fees may apply).

Note: Nike reserves the right to refuse returns that do not meet the above conditions.
